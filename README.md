# The Cinema 3D Horror Game

A video game developed for personal and experimental purposes. The idea for the game was conceived and developed during my second year of my Video Game Programming Engineering degree at UNIAT as a personal, non-academic project.

## Description
An atmospheric horror game with a narrative focus. You wake up alone in a movie theater, but there's something lurking behind the screen...

## Features
- Core gameplay loop focused on rowing, observing your surroundings, and keeping the boat afloat.
- 3D single-player experience.
- Narrative-driven environmental horror.
- *Aesthetics: Low-saturation color palette, and restrained lighting focused on mood and environmental storytelling.
- *Basic enemy AI focused on player pursuit and environmental navigation.
